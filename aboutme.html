About Me

Who am I?

I am an Indonesian who grew up in Shanghai, China and Bangkok, Thailand, currently studying chemical engineering and computer science at the University of California Berkeley. I have internship experience at Coca-Cola Thailand and W.R.Grace Thailand, consulting experience for WeWork Berkeley, and experience co-founding Wristband International. My professional interests are cleantech startups, green technology, polymer research, and innovations in sustainable and renewable energy related fields.

Passions?

My two main passions are football (soccer) and entrepreneurship. I love playing and watching football (soccer), I'm a lifelong Chelsea FC supporter and especially enjoy playing futsal. I'm also addicted to entrepreneurship, which to me is really just enjoying the process of ideating and improving things - anything from small changes to daily living to hopefully in the future tackling some of the world's largest problems. In my spare time I can also be found playing volleyball, saxophone, going to the gym, hiking, travelling with friends or eating at Thai Noodle 2.
