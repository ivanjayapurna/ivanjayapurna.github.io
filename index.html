Who Am I?

Hi I'm Ivan. I was born in Jakarta, Indonesia, spent time living in Shanghai, China and Bangkok, Thailand before moving to the US, where I now live. I am currently a student of computer science and chemical engineering at University of California, Berkeley. I never skip breakfast and wish I had the same dedication to working out.

I like building cool stuff with code, and have experience working on various computational projects in materials science, biochemistry, robotics and art. I hope to one day build something that will save the world.

In my spare time I am an avid football (soccer) player and fan, I can talk Chelsea Football Club or the English Premier League all day. I also like new experiences, doing creative things and going on adventures.
