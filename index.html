About Me

PhD Candidate @ UC Berkeley.

I'm currently working towards solving our global plastic waste problem by researching & developing truly biodegradable plastics. I aspire to be an applied scientist who can write code to do unsupervised learning & high-throughput robotics, as well as make cool biomaterials in lab with my own two hands.

I've called Bangkok, Shanghai, Indonesia, Berkeley and San Francisco home.

I like building cool things with code and science & am always down for a new adventure.

I've spent way too much of my life following Chelsea Football Club.

I don't do much social media but I write stuff on Medium sometimes. I also record a weekly podcast with my friends Wing & Andrei. We talk about life, philosophy, science, technology, venture capital and whatever else tickles our fancy. Check us out on YouTube or Spotify!
